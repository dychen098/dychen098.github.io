lets talk about the importance of sleep

Poor sleep is linked to gaining weight. In recent studies those who didn't get enough sleep were more likely to gain weight. Sleep deprivation causes irregular hormone release which may lead to bigger appetites which can lead to over eating. Thanks to another study, we now know that a limited amount of sleep can lead to the brain functioning akin to being under the influence of alcohol. Like most people have heard, a good night's sleep does in fact imporve problem-solving skills and memory performance. During sleep, we as humans produce proteins known as cytokines, which the body relies on to fight infection, illness, and stress. There are some people that take naps in order to attempt to catch back up on the amount of sleep that they lost, this has been proven to help with a temporary boost of alertness, this cannot however provide the other benefits of actual sleep at night.

Easy ways to help fall asleep faster

  • Don't take naps during the day
  • limit caffine intake
  • Don't look at a clock
  • visualize things that make you happy
  • avoid blue light several hours before attempting to sleep
