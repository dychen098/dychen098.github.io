lets talk about the importance of sleep in kids

Poor sleep is linked to gaining weight. In recent studies children who didn't get enough sleep were 89% more likely to gain weight than those who did get enough sleep. Researchers have learned that sleep helps to protect young growing children from vascular damage. As previously stated sleep helps deal with weight gain, young kids who don't sleep very much are more likely to gain weight and be obese. Although there is no technical research to back this up, kids who sleep more are less likely to get injured, as they are asleep and not walking around. There is some research that links a lack of sleep in 3 year olds to the increasedd chance of them having hyperactivity and impulsivity problems as they age.
